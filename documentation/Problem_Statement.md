# Problem Statement

## Background

Industrial environments such as manufacturing plants, chemical factories, warehouses, and processing units often contain hazardous conditions including gas leaks, poor air quality, high temperatures, smoke, and unsafe working zones. Most inspections still rely on fixed sensors or manual patrols, which cannot provide continuous coverage of every location.

## Existing Challenges

- Manual inspections expose workers to hazardous environments.
- Fixed monitoring systems cannot detect hazards in every area.
- Dangerous conditions may remain unnoticed between inspection intervals.
- Emergency alerts may be delayed.
- Continuous 24/7 monitoring is difficult using only human operators.
- Many industries lack an autonomous inspection solution that combines mobility, AI, and real-time monitoring.

## Proposed Solution

SENTINEL-X addresses these challenges by introducing an AI-powered autonomous industrial safety patrol robot. The robot continuously patrols industrial environments, collects environmental sensor data, performs embedded AI-based risk analysis, provides immediate alerts to nearby workers, streams live data to a dashboard, and automatically returns to a charging dock when its battery is low before resuming its patrol.

## Expected Impact

- Improve worker safety.
- Reduce human exposure to hazardous environments.
- Enable continuous 24/7 monitoring.
- Provide faster hazard detection.
- Support smarter industrial safety management.
