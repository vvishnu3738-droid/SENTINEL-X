# Project Objective

## Project Title

SENTINEL-X – AI Autonomous Industrial Safety Patrol Robot

## Primary Objective

To develop an AI-powered autonomous industrial safety patrol robot using the Arduino UNO Q platform that continuously monitors hazardous industrial environments, detects safety risks, alerts workers in real time, and autonomously returns to a charging dock before resuming its patrol.

## Secondary Objectives

- Perform autonomous industrial patrol.
- Monitor environmental conditions continuously.
- Apply embedded AI for hazard detection.
- Provide real-time worker alerts.
- Display live monitoring data.
- Support uninterrupted 24/7 operation.
- Reduce human exposure to hazardous environments.
